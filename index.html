Intelligent Transportation Lab


The Chongqing Key Laboratory of Spatio-Temporal Information in Mountainous Cities, based at Chongqing Jiaotong University. It is dedicated to addressing complex traffic issues in mountainous cities and promoting the research and development and application services of spatio-temporal data products for typical scenarios in mountainous cities.
